Darkseid

Origins

Darkseid is the tyranical ruler of the planet Apokolips. He travels across the Multiverse to conquer other planets with his alien army. He has beaten many foes but cannot defeat the justice league.

About

Darkseid is a God. He defeated the Old Gods and stole their powers. He uses these powers to wreak havoc across the multiverse and decimate all in his way.

Skills & Traits

  • Omega Rays

    • Darkseid is capable of firing heat seeking blasts from his eyes that lock onto his targets and do not miss. These beams can even harm superman and can keep up with speedsters. They are capable of destroying planets.

  • Superhuman Strength

    • As he is a God, Darkseid has incredible amounts of strength. He is capable of rivaling the strength of superman and others with super strength. His strength is easily able to destroy the constructs created by the Green Lantern.

  • Leadership

    • Darkseid is a ruler of his own planet. He holds the army of an entire race at his disposal and uses his power to make others submit to his will.

  • Erosion Blasts

    • Darkseid is capable of channeling his power to his fists to cause mass amounts of damage to the surrounding area, eradicating most things in his path.
