Deathstroke

Origins

Deathstroke is the world's deadliest assassin. He has been linked with most of the Heroes from the DC universe in more than one scenario. He was the main antagonist in the children's tv show Teen Titans. He is also a recurring character on the show Arrow. Deathstroke does not view his persona as a villain but as a necessary evil.

About

Deathstroke is a mercenary for hire that constantly finds himself at odds with the heroes of the DC universe. He is well versed in many areas of combat and is well able to hold his own against the likes of Batman and Green Arrow, who have both trained to the peak.

Skills & Traits

  • Swordsmanship

    • Deathstroke is a master swordsman. One of his favourite weapons is to use is a large broadsword. He uses this along with his arsenal of weapons that he carries around with him.

  • Mixed Martial Arts

    • Deathstroke is a force to be reckoned with as he not only has incredible weapon skills but also a vast repetoire of martial arts knowledge. Deathstroke can go punch for punch with Batman and has said that he can beat Batman in a few moves.

  • Leadership

    • Deathstroke commands his own army of mercenaries and uses them to cause chaos. He has used this army to raid cities and even to challenge the great Ra's Al Ghul for the leadership of the league of assassins.
