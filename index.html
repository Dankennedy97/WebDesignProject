Green Arrow

Origins

The Green Arrow, or Oliver Queen, began as a rich teenager who was marooned on an island called: Starfish Island. He spent five years on this island and developed the skills he now uses to defend his city, Star city. As a kid Oliver's hero was Robin Hood, and was said to be a natural archer. Oliver frequently practiced with his bow and arrows to improve his skill. His Mother and Father were mauled in a tragic safari accident and was then raised by his uncle, and later inherited the family fortune. He became a typical billionaire playboy that enjoyed spending money rather than earning it. It was only when he was marooned on the island did he begin to change.

About

The Green Arrow is portrayed as a witty, easy going superhero. He is usually depicted as a man of average height with blond hair and a goatee. He is one of the more light hearted heroes in contrast with the likes of batman and uses his arrows in any way he can imagine.

Skills & Traits

  • Weapons Mastery

    • The Green Arrow is an expert with a bow and arrow (i.e. BETTER THAN HAWKEYE). He idolized the character Robin Hood from the kid's stories and ever since childhood has had a keen interest in Archery. This lead him to develop his skills as an archer to the degree that it is at now. Green Arrow is also well versed in using guns and swords as well but prefers to use his bow.

  • Peak Physical Condition

    • The Green Arrow has to be in top shape to be able to fight crime, and he uses many techniques to maintain his physical level.
