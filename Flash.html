The Flash

Origins

There have been a few people, or meta-humans, who have gone by the alias Flash in the dc universe. My personal favourite and the one being discussed on this page is Barry Allen, the second Flash. Barry started off as a CSI working at Central City Police, the city Flash protects. One day, while working in his lab Barry was struck by lightning and fell into a coma for 9 months. When he awoke he found that he could move faster than ever before, becoming the fastest man alive.

About

Barry Allen is the second Flash. He decided that he wanted to be a hero because of the man who took him in when his mother was murdered by his father. This Flash has an easy going personality and is one of the more popular heroes created by DC.

Skills & Traits

  • Time Travel

    • By using his speed, the Flash can travel so fast that he can travel through time. He does not use this ability often as doing so could have serious consequences. This ability can also be used to travel through dimensions otherwise known as the multiverse.

  • Superhuman Speed

    • It's in the name; The Flash. He moves around at lightning fast speeds and uses his powers to fight the crime in central city.

  • Phasing

    • By vibrating his body at incredible speeds, the Flash is able to temporarily make his body intagible so that he can move through solid objects. The Flash has used this ability in many fights with the villains in his universe. There is a drawback to this ability though, if he stops moving while in phase, his molecules will bond with those of the walls and he will die.

  • Electrokinesis

    • When the Flash moves, he creates yellow lightning that follows him around. The Flash is able to use this lightning against his foes.
