Superman

Origins

Superman was born on the planet of Krypton. He was sent to Earth by his parents after they discovered that the planet was dying. He crash landed in a small town named smallville. He was found and adopted by the Kents. His human name is Clark Kent. His Kryptonian name is Kal-El. Clarks story begins in smallville but he doesnt become Superman until he enters into his adult years.

About

Some people refer to Superman as a 'cheat' of a Superhero as he is near unstoppable. That's fair but the reason I have included Superman in this website is not for his monstrous power, it's because of his ideals and willpower. To me Superman is one of the greatest heroes created because no matter how much power he may have he still follows the rules set in place by the law.

Skills & Traits

  • Flight

    • Due to the fact the Superman draws his power from the sun he is granted incredible abilities. One of these abilities is flight. The sun's rays make his body adapt to the gravity of Earth in a strange way.

  • Superhuman Strength

    • The sun affects Supermans body differently than it would someone originally from Earth. The sun increases the muscle mass in his body which gives him super strength.

  • Super Speed

    • Superman can move at speeds close to the flash because of the yellow sun's influence on his body. He once raced the flash across the globe to see who really was the fastest man alive.

  • Heat Vision

    • Superman is capable of producing rays of heat from his eyes that can easily burn through his foes. In the tv show smallville, Superman usually uses his heat vision in creative ways to save the day.
