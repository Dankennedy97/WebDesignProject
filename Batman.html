Batman

Origins

Batman's origin began the night of his parents death. After witnessing the tragedy at such a young age he became obsessed with the justice system in Gotham city and how corrupt it was. Batman trained with some of the world's best fighters to get to the level of skill that he is at and he has learned so many different styles that he is nearly unpredictable in a fight. He is renowned as the world's greatest detective.

About

Batman is the classic vigilante. His methods make him a criminal in the eyes of the police but if he wasn't there Gotham would be in ruins at this point. Batman is cold and calculating and does not trust easily if at all.

Skills & Traits

  • Detective Skills

    • Batman is hailed as the world's greatest detective, even once in a cartoon episode was compared to Sherlock Holmes. Batman is a great detective as he is incorruptible. He may be a little extreme in his methods but there is no questioning the fact that he is a great detective.

  • Intellegence

    • Batman has only survived as long as he has because he has outsmarted all of his foes. In the Justice League war Batman threatens Darkseid with genocide and gives the rest of the league the chance to arrive and stop him from destroying Earth.

  • Gadgets

    • As Batman has no powers beyond what any normal man could achieve, he has to be diverse with his crime fighting ways. Since he is a billionaire, he can fund many of the expensive gadgets that he has made. Batman uses gadgets to either intimidate or to apprehend criminals.

  • Peak Physical Condition

    • Batman is like the Green Arrow in that he is in the best shape a regular man can be in. Batman has trained with many prominent figures in the DC universe and has gotten to the peak of human fitness through this training. He regularly pushes himself past his limits to increase his strength and stamina.
