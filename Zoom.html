Zoom

Origins

At a young age Zoom witnessed his father killing his mother. This left him unhinged and in a very dangerous mental state. He was then granted his super speed by the speedforce, the energy that the flash and other speedsters received their speed from. The fact that Zoom had super speed and also the fact that he was insane left him with this desire to rule.

About

Zoom is like the flash in many ways but there are big differences to their characters. Zoom has an obsession with being the fastest man alive and will stop at nothing to get to that point.

Skills & Traits

  • Super Speed

    • Zoom is the Flash's nemesis, so obviously he would have super speed. Zoom is constantly looking for ways to improve his speed and has even resorted to trying to leech the Flash's speed.

  • Martial Arts

    • Along with having super speed, Zoom is also very skilled in hand to hand combat. He has martial arts skills and has proven that even in a fist fight he can hold his own against the DC heroes.

  • Time Remnants

    • Zoom travels through time and takes out past versions of himself and uses them in fights to either be in two places at once or to scope out a situation before he goes in.

  • Superhuman Reflexes

    • Due to the fact that Zoom has super speed he also has superhuman reflexes and uses them to help him gain an edge in fights.
