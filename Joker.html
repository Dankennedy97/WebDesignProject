The Joker

Origins

*UNKOWN* There is no one story of where the Joker originated. Some say he was a mental patient that was used in experiments and others have deduced that he is somehow linked to the Lazarus pit, what Ra's Al Ghul uses to retain his youth and live for many years. The Joker loves to drive his victims insane and he sometimes makes up some stories as to where he came from to intimidate them. His favourite story is that he was raised by his abusive Aunt and she used to wash his skin in bleach and left him with irreparable brain damage.

About

The Joker is a sociopath that loves to cause havoc across Gotham city. His favourite past time is toying with Batman, trying to get him to break his one rule of not killing anyone.

Skills & Traits

  • Intimidation

    • The Joker intimidates his followers into working for him. He is also a very intimidating villain in the Gotham city as most of the civilians in Gotham fear the Joker the most out of all the villains.

  • Disguie

    • The Joker is great at disguising himself and uses these skills to get into most places that have restricted access. He often disguises himself as police officers to get away with a lot of his schemes.

  • Escapology

    • The Joker is constantly escaping from captivity very quickly after he has been arrested. The Joker has proven many times that Arkham Asylum is not equipt to house him along with many of the other villains imprisoned there.

  • Intellegence

    • The Joker has outsmarted nearly all of the Gotham city criminal bosses, including thugs like TwoFace nd Penguin but that is not his most impressive feat. The Joker has proven many times that he is even able to outsmart Batman with some of his plans.
